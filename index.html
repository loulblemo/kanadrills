Privacy Policy for KanaDrills App

Last Updated: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "KanaDrills" mobile application (the "App") handles information. Your privacy is important to us.

1. No Collection of Personal Information

The KanaDrills App is designed to be a simple, standalone study tool. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, location data, device identifiers, or any other information that could directly identify you.

2. No Account Creation or Login

The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal credentials to use its features. All flashcard functionalities are accessible without any form of registration.

3. No Usage Data Collection

We do not collect any usage data, analytics, or statistics about how you use the KanaDrills App. Your learning progress and interaction with the flashcards remain entirely private on your device.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no user data to share with third parties. The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or other services that would collect or transmit your information.

5. Children's Privacy

The KanaDrills App is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Given that we do not collect any personal information, the App complies with privacy regulations designed to protect children, such as the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
